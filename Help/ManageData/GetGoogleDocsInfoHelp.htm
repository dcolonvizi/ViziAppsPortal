Your phone or iPad can directly interface with spreadsheets in your Google Docs, using visually created query commands. The query commands include insert, update, select and delete.

These commands will run when you tap a button or table row on your phone or iPad. 

To enable a connect, your app needs to know the name of the spreadsheet and the credentials to your Google Docs account.

  • Enter your Google Docs Spreadsheet name and the account Username and Password
  •  Click Save and then click Close
