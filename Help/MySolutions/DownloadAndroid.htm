To download ViziApps on an Android phone:

  • Before you download ViziApps to your phone, first create an online ViziApps account to use our ViziApps Studio. Then prepare a template app in your account to try it out and select that app for testing on the provision page. This will enable you to run the app on your phone.
  • On the Android phone, in the applications listing, tap the Play Store. Your phone must support Android V. 2.2+ to work with ViziApps. If you cannot find it in the Play Store download it here.
  • Tap the search button.
  • Enter ViziApps.
  • Follow the rest of the instructions to install ViziApps on the phone.

  • To see your template app running on your phone, open ViziApps on the phone and login with the same credentials as your ViziApps Studio account credentials. If you have selected an app for testing on the Publish page of our ViziApps Studio then that app will be downloaded to your phone and run immediately.
