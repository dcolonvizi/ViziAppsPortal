The Storyboard feature helps you better organize your app design so you can do it faster. It lets you see all your app pages at once while you work on any one page, giving you a better sense of context and a better sense of page flow.
 

To use the Storyboard just click on View Storyboard and the Storyboard view will pop up from the main design window. 

The Storyboard shows all the pages and associated field names. When the mouse is over a button (right arrow), it hightlights the button and which page the button will show  

next (center arrow).

As you move your mouse over any of the field names on the Storyboard, the field location in the picture of the page is highlighted. In addition, if your mouse is over a button, table or switch where an push action can occur, there will be a connection to the page that the user will see next when they tap the field.
